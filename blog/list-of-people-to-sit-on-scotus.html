List of People that could sit on SCOTUS

15/02/2016

Since Antonin Scalia's death on the 13th, the media talking heads and politicos have been in a flutter with a various ideas of whom Obama will nominate to the High Court. These ideas range from the possible to the bizarre. Here are the names that seen so far in no particular order. As more names roll in, I will update the list.
