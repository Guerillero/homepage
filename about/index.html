I am a Wikipedian, cartographer, public policy junkie, and code monkey. My favorite thing to do is to make maps, charts, and websites that help people understand little-known parts of the world around them. I work for The DHS Program (vis-à-vis ICF) making maps and writing code.

On Wikipedia, I serve as an administrator and a functionary. My major contributions to the encyclopedia include articles about punk rock, protesting nuns, and folk songs. My username is Guerillero.

For a while, I maintained a blog on this domain. Most all of the content was the cringy wrtings of a college student. The posts exist in the etheral of the internet archive, but I am no longer hosting them. The content of my musings were my own, at the time, and do not speak for my my employer(s), my school(s), Wikipedia, or any other person, body, or organizations. I'm honestly not sure if they even speak for me anymore.
